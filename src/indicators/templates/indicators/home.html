About
Who are we?

PeakLife is an innovative service aimed at comparing and discovering in detail the quality of life in every country on the planet. It generates data based on the general country data provided by the world health organisation.



How it works
What do the results mean?

Our algorithm takes into account an extensive collection of factors affecting health and quality of life, in order to give you a complete overview for every country. You can also use PeakLife to find out which country has the best quality of life specifically for you!

The general country score is the weighted score of that particular country against all the other countries in the world. This score indicates the overal grade the quality of life gets in your specific country.

The user tailored score is the weighted score of your particular gender and age group inside that country.

The total country score is the user tailored score and the general country score weighted, so this is your totally personalized score.
